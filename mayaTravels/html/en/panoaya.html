Panoaya's Ranch

  1. Maya Travels
  2. Next Trips
  3. Panoaya's Ranch

Date: January 6
Cost: $ 450 (mexican Pesos) P/P *

We will go to the colorful town of Amecameca, where we will know the Panoaya Ranch, a cultural, fun and ecological park, where the poetess Sor Juana Ines de la Cruz lived.
It dates from the 17th century and has a fun labyrinth and other activities.

Includes

  • Round transportation in Bus or tourist vans
  • Visit and entrance to the Panoaya Hacienda
  • Caressing deers Park
  • Alpine zip line
  • Pedal boats
  • Birds, eagles and wild felines Show
  • Aviary
  • Cyclopath
  • British labyrinth
  • Cheese workshop
  • Volcano workshop
  • Organic garden
  • Semi Olympic pool
  • Sor Juana and Volcano Museums
  • Kids gift
  • Box lunch on board
  • Group guide

Starting point: Xochimilco Sports Park 6:15 a.m. and Chabacano subway station 7:00 a.m.
Return point: 9:00 p.m., Approximate time, at the same points.
