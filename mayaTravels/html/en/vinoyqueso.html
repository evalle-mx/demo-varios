Wine and Cheese Route

  1. Maya Travels
  2. Next Trips
  3. Wine and Cheese Route

Date: January 27
Cost: $ 590 (mexican Pesos) P/P *

We will take you to explore the beautiful magical town of Bernal, to admire its Peña, the third largest monolith in the world, after that we'll taste delicious artisan cheeses, uncork a regional wine in a friendly meeting.

Includes

  • Round transportation in Bus or tourist vans
  • Entrance, visit and tasting to cheese farm
  • Visit and entrance to vineyard with tasting and impressed drinking glass included
  • Visit to Bernal Pueblo Mágico
  • Box lunch on board
  • Group guide

Starting point: Xochimilco Sports Park 6:15 a.m. and Chabacano subway station 7:00 a.m.
Return point: 9:00 p.m., Approximate time, at the same points.
