We invite you to explore a splendid region of beautiful landscapes and lush vegetation, we'll navigate incredible rivers and appreciate the spectacular waterfalls of Tamasopo and Tamul, we will also know one of the 13 Wonders of Mexico, the Sotano de las Golondrinas, one of the most beautiful abysses and deep in the world, where thousands of birds make this place, a spectacular show when they fly out in a perfect spiral.
After We will visit the Magical Town of Xilitla, with its Surrealist Garden, designed by Sir Edward James, a mystic place full of sculptures and magic symbols that decorate this paradisiacal place.
