Valle de Bravo

  1. Maya Travels
  2. Next Trips
  3. Valle de Bravo

Date: February 18
Cost: $ 500 (mexican Pesos) P/P *

Spend a fun Sunday in this colorful Magic Town of the State of Mexico, in which we will appreciate the beautiful waterfall of Velo de Novia which has a drop of 35 meters, then we will board our yacht to navigate this beautiful lake framed by pine and picturesque forests constructions, we will make a tour of the Magical Valle de Bravo, with its cobbled streets, in the Plaza de los Tres Árboles, the main point of the town, it will be a magnificent place to taste rich snows or some delicious skis prepared Vallesano style, in addition to touring Portals where numerous shops offer their services and crafts.

Includes

  • Round transportation in Bus or tourist vans
  • Guided Tour in the magical town of Valle de Bravo
  • One hour yacht Tour
  • Visit to Velo de Novia waterfall
  • Walking and shopping Free time
  • Box lunch on board
  • Group guide

Starting point: February 17, Xochimilco Sports Park 7:15 a.m. and Chabacano subway station 8:00 a.m.
Return point: 10 p.m., Approximate time, at the same points.
