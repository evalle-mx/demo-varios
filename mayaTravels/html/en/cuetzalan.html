Cuetzalan Pueblo Mágico

  1. Maya Travels
  2. Next Trips
  3. Cuetzalan Pueblo Mágico

Date: From February 3 to 5
Cost: Since $ 3,400 (mexican Pesos) P/P *

We will visit the Magic Town of Cuetzalan, located in the foothills of the northern sierra of Puebla.
Place of fertile lands, jungles with gigantic ferns, waterfalls and pools of crystal clear water, come and see their churches, walk through its streets, enjoy its crafts, coffee, embroideries, flowers, huaraches, eat delicious with your food and enjoy the unparalleled show of the Dance of the Papantla Flyers.

Includes

  • Round transportation in Bus or tourist vans
  • Two lodging nights
  • Two continental breakfasts
  • Las Hamacas waterfall visit
  • Las brisas waterfall visit
  • Visit and entrance to the cave the corals
  • Wax Museum and waist loom
  • Guided tour and entrance to the archaeological zone of Yohualichan
  • Guided tour of the jarritos church
  • All places entrance tickets
  • Box lunch on board
  • Group guide

Starting point: February 3, Xochimilco Sports Park 5:00 a.m. and Chabacano subway station 5:30 a.m.
Return point: February 5, 10 p.m., Approximate time, at the same points.
