Monarch butterflies and Valle de Bravo Town

  1. Maya Travels
  2. Next Trips
  3. Monarch butterflies and Valle de Bravo Town

Date: January 13
Cost: $ 500 (mexican Pesos) P/P *

We will take you to the Monarch Butterfly Sanctuary, located 25 km from Valle de Bravo, in this beautiful place, every year millions of Monarch butterflies arrive that travel more than 5,000 km from Cánada, enjoying nature and its beautiful landscapes, then we'll visit the colorful Pueblo Mágico (magical town) of Valle de Bravo.

Includes

  • Round transportation in Bus or tourist vans
  • Entrance and guided tour in the monarch butterfly sanctuary
  • Visit to the bridal veil waterfall
  • Visit to the magical town of Valle de Bravo
  • Box lunch on board
  • Group guide

Starting point: Xochimilco Sports Park 6:15 a.m. and Chabacano subway station 7:00 a.m.
Return point: 9:00 p.m., Approximate time, at the same points.
